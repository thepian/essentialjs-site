About Fresh Translation

Fresh Translation is a newly started company founded by Caterine Apruzzese and its aim is to provide a translation service that is accessible and tailored to our customers' specific needs. Originally from Venezuela, I lived and studied in the city of Oporto in Portugal for ten years and in 2007 I moved to London to complete my education.
